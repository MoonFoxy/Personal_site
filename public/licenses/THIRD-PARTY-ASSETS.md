## Jeelh Retro Cursors

- Author page: <https://jeelh.itch.io/retro-cursor>.
- Source bundle: supplied by the project owner from the author's download.
- Files used without conversion: the light and dark normal, select, text and
  move `.cur` files.
- The supplied bundle and author page do not state a licence for reuse or web
  embedding. Obtain the author's permission or replace this set before a
  public production release.

The cursors preserve their native embedded hotspots. They are used only on
fine-pointer devices; touch, forced-colors and increased-contrast modes retain
the system cursor.
